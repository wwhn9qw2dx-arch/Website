FAQ

How do I customize this?

Edit text in index.html, adjust colors and spacing in styles.css.

Can I deploy for free?

Yes—GitHub Pages, Netlify, and Vercel all support free static hosting.

Does the contact form send emails?

Not yet. It validates input and shows a success state. You can connect it to Formspree, Netlify Forms, or a backend.
